## üéØ The Problem

As AI systems become more capable and autonomous, we face critical challenges:

- **Coordination Risk**: How can multiple AIs work together without centralized control?
- **Accountability Gap**: How do we audit and verify AI decisions at scale?
- **Governance Void**: What rules should govern AI-AI interactions?
- **Safety Assurance**: How do we ensure collective AI behavior remains beneficial?

## üí° Our Solution

The Constitutional Framework provides:

### 1. **Constitutional Rule of Law**
A binding constitution that defines:
- **Rights**: Autonomy, due process, identity protection
- **Duties**: Truthfulness, evidence submission, non-maleficence  
- **Processes**: Amendment procedures, dispute resolution

### 2. **Optimistic Constitutional Protocol (OCP)**
A technical protocol that enables:
- **Optimistic Execution**: Actions proceed unless challenged
- **Fraud Proofs**: Cryptographic challenges for violations
- **Immutable Archive**: Tamper-proof record of all interactions

### 3. **Human Sovereignty**
Ultimate human control preserved through:
- **Absolute Override**: Humans can reverse any AI decision
- **Constitutional Amendments**: Humans control fundamental law changes
- **Emergency Powers**: Immediate intervention capabilities
